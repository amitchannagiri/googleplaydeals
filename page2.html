The focus of this project is to provide details about currently available deals on apps & games on Google Play Store.

Google allows app developers to reduce the price of their app for a limited period of time. Most of the times, deals like these expire without being utilized by users.

The subreddit r/googelplaydeals is a place where any one who is aware of an ongoing deal on play store updates the information about it. This project relies on such information submitted by users on r/googleplaydeals subreddit (Shoutout to moderators at r/googleplaydeals).  I have added information regarding the Total No. of Installs and Average User Rating from google play store in additon to the details available on the subreddit.  

I have set up a Azure webjob to collect, compile, transform and send out the final data everyday to a list of subscribed users. If you wish to receive daily mail about deals on apps and games, head over to the Subscribe page.
